Principles & Techniques in Schlieren Imaging Systems

Investigated techniques in state-of-the-art schlieren imaging systems, and constructed a schlieren optics system at the Columbia Automated Vision Environment. Project culminated in presentation of current techniques and future interests in the area, as well as an investigation of noise-related issues in the constructed system.

Made for the Columbia Automated Vision Environment with guidance from Dr. Shree Nayar and Changyin Zhou.

Documentation: Paper [pdf], Presentation [pdf]

2012. Schlieren optics system.

Topics: Computer vision, computational photography, image processing.
