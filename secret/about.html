About Me


I'm a student at Columbia University, studying computer engineering and
comparative literature. This is my personal space to create and share.


My research interests are in computer vision and image processing, and
I tinker with photography and design on the side.


I'm an undergraduate researcher at the Columbia Automated Vision Environment,
and I freelance photograph for campus publications and performance groups.


About the Site


This site is hosted & published by Github Pages.
The blog is hosted & published by Tumblr.

Text is set in Futura & FF Tisa, powered by Typekit.


Everything is homemade in TextMate or TextEdit on an
Apple 13" unibody Macbook Pro.
I use NValt and Writer to draft and edit posts, and Photoshop to handle photos.

Content is fueled by spinach and smoked salmon.
